Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about One-Click Craft

How does One-Click Craft work?

One-Click Craft analyzes job postings and your resume, then uses AI to generate tailored suggestions for resume improvements, cover letters, and answers to application questions - all within a convenient browser extension.

What makes One-Click Craft different from other resume tools?

Unlike most resume tools, One-Click Craft integrates directly with your browser so you can tailor your application while viewing the job posting. It's context-aware and provides job-specific suggestions, not generic advice.

How many job applications can I use One-Click Craft for?

One-Click Craft uses a credit system. You get 10 free credits when you install. Each job application uses 1 credit (covering resume suggestions, cover letter, and multiple application questions). Additional credits can be purchased at any time.

Is my data secure when using One-Click Craft?

Yes. Your resume and documents are stored locally in your browser's storage. We don't permanently store your resume or personal information on our servers. All data processing is encrypted and secure.

What file formats does One-Click Craft support?

One-Click Craft supports PDF, DOCX, and TXT resume files. You can download your generated cover letters in both DOCX and PDF formats for easy submission.
